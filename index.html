COMPANY OVERVIEW

What is RARITONE?

Raritone is an AI-powered virtual try-on & shopping platform that helps fashion brands, designers, and boutiques increase sales both online and in-store.

Customers can see how outfits fit on their own body before buying — removing guesswork around size & fit.

Key Features

  • Smartphone body scanning technology
  • Realistic virtual fitting experience
  • Accurate size recommendations
  • Higher conversion rates & fewer returns
  • Designed for Indian body types

Target Market

  • Boutiques & Fashion Designers
  • D2C Clothing Brands
  • Retail Chains
  • Fashion E-commerce Platforms

MISSION, VISION & VALUES

Mission

To revolutionize fashion shopping by eliminating size guesswork through AI-powered virtual try-on technology, making shopping confident, convenient, and personalized for every body type.

Vision

To become the standard virtual fitting solution for the global fashion industry, bridging the gap between online and offline shopping experiences.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Continuously advancing AI fitting technology
  • Inclusivity: Designing for diverse body types
  • Partnership: Growing together with vendors
  • Confidence: Making shopping stress-free
  • Sustainability: Reducing returns and fashion waste
